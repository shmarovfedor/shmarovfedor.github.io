Fedor Shmarov

I am a Research Associate in ICOS group, School of Computing at Newcastle University. My background is in model checking and formal verification of parametric hybrid systems. Currently I am a part of the project where we develop personalised ultraviolet (UVB) treatment of psoriasis. My main responsibilities include mathematical modelling of UVB therapy for psoriasis and analysis of clinical data using machine learning and statistical methods. [Academic CV]
